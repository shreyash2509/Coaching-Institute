About Us

The Mercy School

Mercy school, New Delhi, a non-profit making society registered under the society's Registration Act of 1860. The members of the society are renowned Educationists, Jurists, Engineers and Administrators. Mercy school, delhi has successfully replicated the excellence that the other Mercy schools in India and abroad have shown in providing holistic education to its students.We believe in the dignity and worth of each student and recognise the importance of his/her responsibilities to the world community. We believe that all youth should have equal opportunities for education, consistent with their individual capabilities and with their personal and social needs.Apart from academics, DPS delhi ensures that the students are provided all opportunities for honing their co-curricular inclinations through sports, performing and visual arts, oratory and other creative pursuits.
